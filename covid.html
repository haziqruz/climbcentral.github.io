Covid-19 Guidelines


Climbing is finally back on the menu!
However, in order to ensure that it stays this way, please adhere to the new guidelines that we have implemented.
We are all in this together.

New Rules in the Gym

To prevent the spread of Covid-19, such rules are necessary for everyone's safety

Mask On

Unless you are climbing, masks are to be worn at all times. That includes when resting or belaying!

Alternate Lanes

Always ensure that there is no one climbing on the lanes beside you to maintain social distancing when climbing. Likewise, please be considerate and wait for your turn or share the lanes with other climbers

Social Distancing

Maximum group of up to 8 people only! Maintain at least 1 meter between each other at all times.
